Stuck in the Seventies

The Rolling Stones

Released in 1981

This fantastic Rolling Stones compilation album was released in 1981 and reached number 15 on the charts in the USA.
It is their fourth compilation album and includes such hits as "Shattered" and "Beast of Burden".

Band Members

Mick Jagger - Lead Vocals
Keith Richards - Guitar
Ronnie Wood - Guitar
Mick Taylor - Guitar
Bill Wyman - Bass
Charlie Watts - Percussion
